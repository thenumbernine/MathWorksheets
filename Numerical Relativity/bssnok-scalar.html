Stress-energy:
$T_{ab} = \Phi_{,a} \Phi_{,b} - \frac{1}{2} g_{ab} g^{cd} \Phi_{,c} \Phi_{,d}$

$\rho = n^a n^b T_{ab}$
$= n^a n^b (\Phi_{,a} \Phi_{,b} - \frac{1}{2} g_{ab} g^{cd} \Phi_{,c} \Phi_{,d})$
$= \Pi^2 + \frac{1}{2} g^{cd} \Phi_{,c} \Phi_{,d}$
$= \Pi^2 + \frac{1}{2} (\gamma^{cd} - n^c n^d) \Phi_{,c} \Phi_{,d}$
$= \Pi^2 + \frac{1}{2} (\gamma^{cd} \Psi_c \Psi_d - \Pi^2)$
$= \frac{1}{2} (\Pi^2 + \gamma^{cd} \Psi_c \Psi_d)$
