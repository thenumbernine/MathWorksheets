$\rho =$ density
$v_i =$ velocity
$m_i = \rho v_i = $ momentum
$P =$ pressure
$\gamma =$ heat capacity ratio
$\tilde\gamma = \gamma - 1$
$E_{kin} = \frac{1}{2} \rho v^k v_k$
$E_{int} = \frac{P}{\tilde\gamma}$
$E_{total} = E_{kin} + E_{int}$
$H_{total} = E_{total} + P = \frac{1}{2} \rho v^2 + \frac{\gamma}{\tilde\gamma} P$
