Navier-Stokes

$(\rho)_{,t} + (\rho v_j)_{,j} = 0$
$(\rho v_i)_{,t} + (\rho v_i v_j + \delta_{ij} P - \tau_{ji})_{,j}= \rho g_j$
$(E_{total})_{,t} + (v_j H_{total} - v_i \tau_{ij} + q_j)_{,j} = 0$
$\tau_{ij} = 2 \mu S_{ij} $

$S_{ij} = \frac{1}{2} ( v_{i,j} + v_{j,i} ) - \frac{1}{3} \delta_{ij} v_{k,k} $
$q_j = -\lambda T_{,j} = -C_p \frac{\mu}{Pr} T_{,j}$
